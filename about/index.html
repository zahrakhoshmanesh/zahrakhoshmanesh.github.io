About me

I am a Ph.D. student of Computer Science at Iowa State University of Science and Technology under supervision of Dr. Robyn Lutz in the Laboratory for Software Safety (LSS) .

Research Interests

My research interests include the principles and applications of Software Engineering, Software Safety, Software Product Line, and Machine Learning. I’m currently focusing on effective and efficient methods to detect and predict Feature Interaction in Software Product line.

Educational Background

I have earned my M. S. degree in Software Engineering from Shiraz University in 2012 and my bachelor in Computer Engineering (Software) from Shahid Chamran University of Ahvaz (former Jondishapur).
